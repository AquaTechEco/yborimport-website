Our Mission

We find family-owned producers who pour their soul into what they make — and bring their work to the world.

Ybor Import Co. exists to champion the small, the unknown, and the exceptional. We partner with family-owned and operated producers — the ones who care more about craft than scale, who've been perfecting recipes for generations, who deserve a stage bigger than their hometown.

Based in Tampa's historic Ybor City — a neighborhood built by immigrants who brought their traditions across the ocean — we carry that same spirit forward. We discover, we import, and we deliver.

Our Story

Born in Ybor City

In the 1880s, immigrants from Cuba, Spain, Italy, and Germany built Ybor City from the ground up. They brought their skills, their recipes, and their refusal to settle for anything less than authentic.

That's the energy we carry. Ybor Import Co. was founded to do what this neighborhood has always done — connect cultures through the things we eat and drink, one family producer at a time.

“We don't discover products. We discover people — and then we tell their story.”
— Ybor Import Co.
